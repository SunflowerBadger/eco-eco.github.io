What are Native Plants?

Native plants have evolved or adapted to local environment for thousands of years, and are an important part of our local habitats, ecosystems and ecosystem services (pollination, infiltration, carbon sequestration, etc). They are the most sustainable plants for a specific area. Native plants not only adapted physically, but chemically and genetically.
